Get your girlfriends together and come enjoy a delicious brunch together for a fantastic cause

Bump, Birth and Baby is back for 2018! This event is all about empowering mums and women, while raising funds for two fabulous organisations - Still Aware and Love Mercy Foundation. We'll hear about these two incredible organisations and how they are empowering women and changing communities.

We'll be sharing brunch & coffee together while hearing from a panel of fabulous speakers. This year we have a focus on health & wellbeing, particularly mental health and how we can survive and thrive through the challenges life and motherhood bring.

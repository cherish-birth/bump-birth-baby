Are you pregnant? Have you recently had a baby? Get your girlfriends together, come enjoy yourselves all for a fantastic cause.

You’ll be treated to canapés on arrival, a scrumptious two course lunch, markets to browse and shop, raffles, a silent auction, games & giveaways, activations (makeup touch ups and photo wall anyone?) and beautiful gift boxes for each guest valued at over $100!! There’ll be a panel of wisdom as we hear from midwives and doulas about birth and postpartum.
